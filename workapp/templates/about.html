how we do it ?

The platfrom will provide the users to enter their skills and qualfication and the platform will then filter out the jobs matching the entered details hereby removing problem of irrelevant applications and unresponsivess.

Since database of all other job platforms are kept in one place i.e. in the platform thus user will not have to register and make profiles again and again on different portals.

  • Web Scraping is used.
  • Removes problems of unresponsive and irrelevant applications.
  • No need to register on different portals.

why we do it ?

The present job market is too much crowded with diffrent job portal thus giving rise to a lot of problems i.e. irrelevant applications, unresponsiveness. If anyone is applying on a job posting listed on a job portal then in most cases it will take you to either on career portal of commpany or some other job board. Thus , one has to register and make profiles again on diffrent career portals which makes the process cumbersome for job seekers. To overcome these problems we have created a platform where all the database of other job portals will kept in one place.
